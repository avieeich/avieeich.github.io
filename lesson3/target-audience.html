Target Audience

This website is designed for adults who live in Idaho who want to be informed about the weather.

Personas

Chelsea is a 32 year-old mother of two children who lives in Fish Haven. She uses the website to see the temperature and weather conditions to determine how warmly she needs to dress her children.

Darren is a 45 year-old resident of Soda Springs, Idaho. He commutes to Preston every day for work and checks this website on his phone to see what conditions will be like.

Scenarios

  • What's the weather like in Soda Springs? The Temperature?
  • Where is the oncoming storm going to hit? Will it pass through Fish Haven?
  • How did the snow storm effect Preston?
  • What do the roads look like in southern Idaho? Is it safe to drive?
  • Is it a warm enough day to go outside?
