Your Adventure Starts Here!

Here at Salmon River Adventures we have three exciting tours, so you can choose the adventure that is perfect for you. We provide all equipment and lunch is on us! Bring an extra layer, water bottle, sunglasses, hat, sunscreen, secure footwear and come ready to make a splash!

Take the Scenic Route

A relaxing float down the river with no rapids, and a warm soak in the hot springs at the end! This is the perfect opportunity to enjoy the beautiful scenery of Idaho, and to observe passing wildlife. $95

Book Online!

Get Your Feet Wet

Our most popular tour! This tour down the class III rapids is perfect for the beginnes and experts alike, making it fun for the whole family! Available for raft or inflatable kayak! $80

Book Online!

The High Adventure

These class IV rapids are fast, fun and thrilling! It's an exciting and challenging adventure for those with a little rafting experience under their belt. $105
